In State of InnDB Online DDL in MySQL 5.6.9-RC (good news included) I found very encouraging online DDL for InnoDB behavior.

I tested ROW_FORMAT manipulation, adding and removing of indexes, adding and removing of columns. All went just well.

But I felt the experiment was incomplete, so I wanted to test the following additional DDL:

  • MODIFY COLUMN: change an existing column from one datatype to another (say INT to BIGINT)
  • ADD CONSTRAINT: add foreign key constraints to a table
  • Repeat all tests on table with constraints
