Asynchronous replication of specific MySQL tables across arbitrary servers -- not related by any MySQL replication topology.

With this tool you can distribute a table to one or more "target" servers

A new tool developed at Outbrain by my colleague Yonatan Maman and myself. Graciously released to open source.
